Services

Vehicle Parts

We offer a wide range of vehicle parts for all categories, from passenger and cargo vehicles to commercial fleets.

We supply original and aftermarket parts directly from the manufacturers, ensuring high quality products at competitive prices.

Industrial Parts

We collaborate with global leaders in industrial equipment manufacturing.

Our managers also specialise in sourcing obsolete and hard-to-find equipment, parts and tools based on individual client requests.

Heavy Equipment Parts

We supply parts from trusted manufacturers for heavy equipment across the engineering, mining, energy, and shipbuilding sectors.

Logistics Services

Our company offers a wide range of logistics services covering all aspects of transportation and supply chain management, including:

> International transportation and the worldwide delivery of goods in compliance with all customs requirements and regulations.

> Warehousing services, with the storage of goods in modern warehouses, with stock optimisation and order picking.

> Express delivery of small shipments in the shortest possible time.

> Customs clearance assistance in obtaining all necessary documents and certificates for the smooth passage of goods across borders.

> Supply chain management through the optimisation of goods delivery processes from the manufacturer to the end consumer.

> Freight tracking with the ability to monitor the movement of goods across all stages of transportation.
